Wait

This was an exercise in frustration for the viewer, limiting me to a minimalist palette of colors. The actionscript was the most challenging part, and is the section I am most proud of. Created in the spring of 2012, using Adobe Flash CS5.

Geotype

This was created in fall of 2011, with corrections and an evenutal complete redo later in the spring of 2012. The original idea was to create an interactive tour of the most common rocks (and minerals) found in the earth. In the spring of 2012, it won Honorable Mention in the New Media Juried Student Show in the category of Interactive Art and Design. Created in the fall of 2011, using Adobe Flash CS5.

Surrealist Website

This website was created for an upper level class at UNC Asheville, and it features the art movement surrealism. It is meant to look like, feature, and act like the movement, so all the interactivity is meant to inspire the surrealist in the viewer. Coded by hand using Sublime Text, in fall 2012.

Fri[n/d]ge Words

This project is an online version of the popular fridge magnets, with a twist. These magnets follow Oulipo's n+7 constraint, and change at random times. Created in fall of 2012, for my interactive class. Hand coded using Sublime Text.

De Stijl

The actionscript I'm particularly proud of has to do with the gallery page, since it was the most difficult to debug. Created in spring 2012, with Adobe Flash CS5.

Song Bars

Realistic Ball

This is one of my older programs, but still one I'm proud of. It is meant to show how a ball falls and settles to the ground realistically. To make the ball fall down the screen, hold down the mouse button. To freeze it in freefall at any time during the drop, stop holding the mouse button down. Created in spring of 2010, in Processing.
